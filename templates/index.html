The dataset

This section covers the dataset the predictions on this page are based on. The dataset is sourced from the Finnish institute for health and welfare (Terveyden ja hyvinvoinnin laitos, THL). THL provides the data used on this page as open data. No modifications to the dataset have been made to facilitate the predictions on this page. All visualizations of the THL data are provided as is with no guarantees of correctness.
